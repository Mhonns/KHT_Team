With experience in project management, communications and fundraising, she has worked with a number of different charities focused on poverty alleviation. She has a Masters in Violence, Conflict and Development from SOAS and a Bachelors in Anthropology and Law from the London School of Economics. Summereen is excited to work with her KHT colleagues and trustees and continue our success!
